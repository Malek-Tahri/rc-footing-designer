
## Design Scope

The project aims to implement the full set of calculations required for isolated footing design according to ACI 318-19 and standard geotechnical verification procedures.

**Soil Pressure and Contact Conditions**
- Elastic pressure distribution
- Biaxial bending with eccentric loading
- Detection of uplift and partial soil contact
- Iterative neutral axis solution for large eccentricity cases

**Geotechnical Verification**
- Bearing capacity verification
- Stability against rotation
- Average settlement estimation
- Differential settlement checks

**Structural Verification (ACI 318-19)**
- One-way shear verification
- Two-way (punching) shear verification
- Bending moment distribution in both directions
- Required reinforcement in top and bottom layers
- Minimum reinforcement requirements

**Quantities**
- Concrete volume
- Formwork area
- Reinforcement quantities

These calculations define the intended engineering scope of the project.

## Possible Future Extensions

After the complete footing design workflow is implemented, additional developments may include:

- Integration with CAD or parametric modeling environments
- Interfaces with structural analysis tools
- Parametric optimization of footing geometry
- BIM-compatible data export

These are separate developments and are not required for the completion of the core design tool.
